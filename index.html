Server Rules

Issue Reporting

Use /report to signal issues. Don't hesitate to report suspicious behavior or harassment.

Realistic RP

Roleplay must be realistic and reflective of the area of play. Avoid unrealistic actions.

No RDM / VDM

Randomly killing or using vehicles to attack others is strictly prohibited.

Active Participation

Stay within Area of Play and engage in roleplay. Avoiding others is prohibited.

Meta / Powergaming

No using outside info (Meta) or manipulating mechanics for forced outcomes (Power).

Communication

No spamming voice/text. Must have a microphone and speak/understand English.

Exploiting

No speed boosting, mod-menus, or glitch abuse. Cheating is not permitted.

Zero Bullying

No hate speech, racism, sexism, or homophobia. Respect all players.

Low Quality RP

No ERP, repeated crime spam, or forcing undesired roleplay on others.

Combat Logging

Do not leave during active roleplay situations. Stay in character near others.

Violating rules can lead to kicks or bans. Rules are to be followed in spirit, not just wording. If you're unclear, contact Server Government.
