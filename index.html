Operations Manual

This document serves as the primary governing text for all law enforcement operations within Dynamic Gaming LE. Every officer is required to maintain a thorough understanding of these procedures.

Use of Force Continuum

Level 1: Presence

The mere presence of an officer is the first level of force.

Level 2: Verbal Commands

Clear, concise verbal directions to the suspect.

Level 3: Less-Lethal

Utilization of Tasers, OC Spray, or Beanbag Shotguns.

Level 4: Lethal Force

Highest level of force, only to be used when there is an immediate threat to life.

Professional Conduct

Officers must maintain a professional demeanor at all times. This includes:

  • Strict adherence to the chain of command.
  • Maintaining radio silence during priority calls.
  • Respectful treatment of civilians and other emergency personnel.
